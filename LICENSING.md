## Dependency note

Two runtime dependencies are themselves AGPL-3.0 and independently licensed
by their owners: [PyMuPDF](https://pymupdf.readthedocs.io/) (Artifex; offers
its own commercial license) and [MinerU](https://github.com/opendatalab/MinerU)
(used for formula-aware PDF extraction). A nexus commercial license does not
cover them; organizations with AGPL policies should evaluate those packages
separately.
